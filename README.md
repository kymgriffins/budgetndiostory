# Budget Ndio Story üåç

A civic storytelling platform making Kenya's national budget accessible and engaging for youth through interactive data visualizations, podcasts, videos, and stories.



## About

Budget Ndio Story bridges the gap between complex government budgets and everyday citizens. We transform fiscal data into compelling narratives using:

- **Budget Tracker** - Interactive visualizations of national and county budgets
- **Stories** - Human-interest stories showing how public funds impact communities
- **Podcasts** - Deep-dive conversations on fiscal policy and governance
- **Shorts** - Quick, engaging videos explaining budget concepts
- **Learn** - Educational resources for budget literacy
